Overview

India is a country in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country by land area, the second-most populous country, and the most populous democracy in the world.

History

The history of India includes the Indus Valley Civilization, Vedic period, Maurya and Gupta empires, and more.

Culture

India has a diverse and rich cultural heritage, including art, music, dance, and various festivals.

Geography

India is characterized by diverse landscapes, including the Himalayan mountain range, fertile plains, and coastal regions.
